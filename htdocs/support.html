Content Disclaimer

This is a third-party website. I am not affiliated with Mojang Studios in any way, shape or form. They are also not paying me in any way, shape or form. I made this website for fun and for Computer Science 3.


All the graphics and information found in this website are based on Mojang Studios' Minecraft, or from sources based on Mojang Studios' Minecraft.


The subject of this website is Minecraft Java Edition version 1.16.1 and this website will not cover anything after that version. For simplicity, this website uses normal difficulty and will not take into account armor nor enchantments. This website will also not include mobs that cannot be naturally encountered in survival mode.
