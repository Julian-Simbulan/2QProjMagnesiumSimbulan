Boss Mobs

Boss mobs are special creatures that are more powerful and dangerous than any other mob. They do not spawn randomly, and approaching them causes a health bar to appear on top of the player's screen. They grant bountiful rewards when defeated.

Ender Dragon

The ender dragon is a giant dragon found in the End dimension, and is often considered as the end-goal of Minecraft playthroughs. She randomly flies around the middle of the main End island, being healed by 10 end crystals on top of obsidian towers, and destroying any block along her way that is not bedrock, obsidian, iron bars nor end stone. She deals different types of attacks, like flicking the player up a few dozen blocks, shooting fireballs with lingering damage, breathing out a deadly purple cloud, and flapping her wings to knockback nearby mobs. Once defeated for the first time, the bedrock structure in the middle opens, which upon entering plays a cutscene and teleports the player to the Overworld. She can be resummoned to be defeated another time. Everytime she dies, an end gateway portal appears near the edge of the End island.

Spawn Conditions: initially spawned in the End; resummoned by placing 4 end crystals on the edges of the bedrock structure

Health: 200 hp (100 x )

Damage:
10 hp (5 x ) from melee
5 hp (2.5 x ) from wings
3 hp (1.5 x ) per sec from dragon's breath
6 hp (3 x ) per sec from dragon fireball

XP Drop: 12,000 XP when killed

Loot Drops:

dragon egg 1 when killed, placed on top of bedrock structure

Fun Fact:
The name of the ender dragon is "Jean".

Wither

The wither boss is a large 3-headed mob that can be summoned anywhere by building a T-shape with soul sand and placing 3 wither skeleton skulls above the T. Upon being summoned, the wither is immune to damage for a few seconds, before exploding and being active. It is immune to fire and drowning damage, actively kills all nearby mobs except undead mobs and ghasts by shooting skulls at them, and break any block it flies through except obsidian and bedrock. When the wither's health is below 50%, it also becomes immune to projectiles. Upon dying it drops a nether star, a useful item that can only be obtained from the wither.

Spawn Conditions: built by player

Health: 300 hp (150 x ), passive regeneration of 1 hp (0.5 x ) per sec until it reaches 300 hp (150 x )

Damage:
96 hp (48 x ) at most upon spawning
8 hp (4 x ) + 1 hp (0.5 x ) every 1 sec for 10 sec

XP Drop: 50 XP when killed

Loot Drops:

nether star 1 when killed
wither rose 1 when the wither's target is killed, placed on the target's location

Fun Fact:
The name of the wither boss is "Simmons".
