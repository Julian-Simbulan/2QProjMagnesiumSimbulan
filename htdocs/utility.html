Utility Mobs

Utility mobs are creatures that can be built by the player. They often assist the player in dealing with other mobs in both an offensive and defensive role.

Iron Golem

Iron golems are large powerful defending mobs found in villages or made by players. They are built by making a T-shaped structure out of iron blocks, then placing a carved pumpkin on top of it. In villages, they patrol the village, offer poppies to villagers, and attack hostile mobs and players that hurt them or the villagers. Outside villages, they wonder randomly, killing hostile mobs nearby. When attacking, they swing their arms up, causing large amounts of damage and their target being flung upwards.

Spawn Conditions: built by player; in villages; in cages in pillager outposts

Health: 100 hp (50 x )

Damage:
12 hp (6 x ) - 16.5 hp (8.25 x ) against endermen
7 hp (3.5 x ) - 21 hp (10.5 x ) against players
7.5 hp (3.75 x ) - 21.5 hp (10.75 x ) against others

XP Drop: 0 XP

Loot Drops:

iron ingot 3-5 when killed
poppies 0-2 when killed

Fun Fact:
Iron golems offering poppies is a reference to the ancient robots in "Laputa: Castle in the Sky".

Snow Golem

Snow golems are weak snow-based mobs that are built by the player. They are made by making a 2-high tower out of snow blocks, then placing a carved pumpkin on top of it. They walk around producing snow along their path, avoid many hazards, and melt in warm biomes and contact with water. They aggravate nearby non-animal neutral and hostile mobs by throwing snowballs at them until the target is dead or out of range. They are most effective against blazes, who get hurt from the snowball thrown against them.

Spawn Conditions: built by player

Health: 4 hp (2 x )

Damage: 3 hp (1.5 x ) against blazes

XP Drop: 1-3 XP when killed

Loot Drops:

snowball 0-15 when killed

Fun Fact:
Snow golems have a hidden face that can be revealed by removing their pumpkin helmet with shears.
